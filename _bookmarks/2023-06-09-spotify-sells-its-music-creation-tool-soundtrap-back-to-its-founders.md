I was in Soundtrap so this was the end of the Spotify campaign for me. I exercised my right under [Section 6b of the Employment Protection Act](https://666a.se/section-6b-of-las-v2022:836-in-english) to oppose the transfer of my employment, but a transfer of business like this one doesn't leave much legal scope after that to avoid redundancy under Swedish employment law.
