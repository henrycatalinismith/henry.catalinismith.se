Over the last couple of months [Zellij](https://zellij.dev/) has become one of
my favourite everyday tools. I'm at a point now where I don't want to be
configuring stuff with millions of custom keybindings. Instead I want something
that works already out of the box. Zellij is that.

To help me learn Zellij's default keybindings I created this printable
cheatsheet. I keep a printed copy on my desk to look stuff up quickly when I
need it.

It's available as [HTML](/2025/11/21/zellij.html), [PNG](/2025/11/21/zellij@1583x1916.webp)
and [PDF](/2025/11/21/zellij.pdf).
