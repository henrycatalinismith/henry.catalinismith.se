[Tailbone: Disk 2](https://henry.catalinismith.com/tailbone/disk2) is a harder
version of the [original Tailbone](https://henry.catalinismith.com/tailbone/).
In the original version, the pace starts off nice and easy-going, and the
difficulty only ramps up properly after you complete the first 10 – 15 combos.
In Disk 2 the difficulty is basically maxed out immediately.

I made this for myself, because I got bored of playing the easy parts. The
harder levels are the stuff I think is the most fun to play in Tailbone, and I
wanted a way to just skip straight to that. I don't actually know if anyone else
will enjoy this or even find it playable at all. It's definitely not going to be
a good way to
[learn how to play Tailbone from scratch](/2022/10/25/tailbone-instructions/).

[Give it a try](https://henry.catalinismith.com/tailbone/disk2) anyway if you're
curious!
