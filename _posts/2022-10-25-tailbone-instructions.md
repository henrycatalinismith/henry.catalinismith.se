## Jump

On a touchscreen device, press the screen once to jump. On a keyboard, the `X`
key triggers a jump. The `space` key also works. Clicking the left mouse button
works too.

## Fly

Instead of releasing your slam straight away, hold down that second jump press
to fly. Flying makes your jump last longer so you can cross larger distances in
the air.

## Combo

Chain together multiple tricks without touching the floor to fill up your combo
bar. Each time you max out the bar you get a score bonus and the difficulty
increases slightly.
